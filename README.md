## 🧪 Background on SBTI results

The SBTI test identifies patterns in how people think and act. Personality science uses these patterns to group users into archetypes. Understanding your archetype provides insight into your communication style and your approach to problems. The 25 standard personalities cover the majority of test takers. The HHHH category serves as a catch-all group for results that do not fit the standard criteria. The DRUNK category serves as an analytical anomaly found in specialized test versions. This wiki documents the traits for each to ensure you have a reference point for your specific test score.
